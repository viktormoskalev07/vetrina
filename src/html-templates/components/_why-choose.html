Why Choose Vetrina Group

We believe the customer journey is #1. Simply put, we believe most consultants focus their attention on one area or department of the business: IT, Operations, Marketing, Branding, People, etc and recommend a ‘one-size-fits-all’ solution no matter who their client is.

At Vetrina Group, we approach each client by first listening to their needs, dig into the data and utilize our expertise before presenting a strategic, effective and practical solution to their problem.

We keep our eye on the big picture and derive the way forward by mapping the journey together ensuring unity and balance for not only the business but their people.

For example, if a retailer has an issue with inventory management, VG knows it is not ONLY an inventory management issue. It is a cocktail of problems consisting of perhaps ordering processes, visual merchandising chaos and lack of sales training. We attack it as such to balance accountability, responsibility, and success across the organization at each touchpoint of a product’s (or customers) journey.
